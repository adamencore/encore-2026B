Our Mission

Our Mission

Encore Performing Arts inspires and empowers young artists to discover and develop important life skills through unparalleled arts education, mentorship, and extraordinary theater experiences.

Through the lens of acting, storytelling, and theater-making, we help young artists grow skills that carry far beyond the stage.

Collaboration & teamwork Critical thinking & problem-solving Giving & receiving feedback Communication Confidence Leadership Empathy
What We Believe

Inspiring. Inclusive. Enriching.

Inspiring

We believe in the transformative power of musical theatre. Whether a student decides to work in the arts or become a doctor, lawyer, engineer, or educator, the creative and collaborative experiences they have through arts education can inspire confidence, innovation, and empathy.

Inclusive

We believe in celebrating the individuality of all people through rich artistic and social experiences. Musical-theatre-based arts education allows everyone involved to share differing perspectives and build connections across communities, backgrounds, and abilities.

Enriching

We believe learning is an eternal process, and that arts-based education allows individuals of all learning styles to grow beyond barriers and prepare for life's possibilities. By providing innovative, imaginative, and enlightening opportunities across all subject areas, Encore helps guarantee the future of our art form.

How we began

Encore was founded by Abigail Pearson, Adam Record & Tara Griffith in 2022.
