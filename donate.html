FAQ

Giving questions

Need a hand or want to give another way? Call (435) 414-0049 or send a note.

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes. Encore Performing Arts is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, so your gift is tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You'll receive a receipt for your records.
Where does my donation go?
Gifts directly fund scholarships, productions, arts education and mentorship, and free, accessible programming such as our sensory-friendly performances — giving young artists in southern Utah a place to grow and shine on stage.
Can I give monthly instead of once?
Yes. You can make a one-time gift or give monthly. Our monthly program, The Core, starts at $25 a month and provides steady support that helps us plan ahead.
Can my company sponsor Encore or give in-kind?
Absolutely. We welcome corporate sponsorships and in-kind donations of goods, services, and volunteer time. Contact us or call (435) 414-0049 to discuss the options.
