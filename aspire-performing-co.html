About Aspire

A show choir for every age

Aspire Performing Co. is Encore's audition-based show choir, where singers and dancers come together as one performing ensemble. Members learn vocal parts and choreography side by side, building the kind of stage presence and teamwork that only comes from performing as a group.

Because Aspire is all-ages, performers from age 8 through high school senior train and perform together, grouped by age and experience. Throughout the season, the company appears at Encore productions, community events, and showcases around southern Utah.

FAQ

Questions about Aspire

Everything families ask about joining the show choir, auditions, and rehearsals.

What is Aspire Performing Co.?
Aspire is Encore's audition-based show choir. Members sing and dance together as a performing ensemble, rehearsing through the season and appearing at Encore productions, community events, and showcases.
Who can join, and what ages does it serve?
Aspire is all-ages, open by audition to performers from age 8 through high school senior. Members are placed into groups appropriate to their age and experience.
How often are auditions held?
Auditions are held twice a year — typically once before the fall season and once before the spring season. Watch this page and Encore's social channels for the next dates.
What should I prepare for an audition?
Prepare a short song to sing, and come in movement-friendly clothing for a brief group dance portion. No monologue is required. Exact requirements are listed with each audition announcement.
Where does Aspire rehearse and perform?
Aspire rehearses and performs at the historic Electric Theater in downtown St. George. Encore is a tenant at the Electric Theater, which is owned and operated by the City of St. George.
Do I need previous experience to audition?
No. Aspire welcomes both newer performers and those with training. The audition simply helps the directors place each member in the right group.

Ready to join Aspire?

Auditions come around twice a year. Register for the next round, or reach out with any questions.
