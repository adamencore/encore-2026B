About the Production

Step into the story

Encore Performing Arts invites young performers ages 12–18 to audition for our production of Anastasia. Step into a story filled with history, mystery, and unforgettable music as we bring it to the stage.

This production offers rich character roles, strong ensemble opportunities, and a chance for young artists to push their skills in acting, singing, and dance. Whether you're an experienced performer or eager to take the next step in your artistic journey, this is a meaningful opportunity to grow, collaborate, and help tell a powerful story about identity, resilience, and hope.

FAQ

Audition questions

Still have questions? Email hello@encorepa.org or contact us.

Who can audition for Anastasia?
Young performers ages 12–18 are invited to audition. Experienced performers and those taking their next step are both welcome.
When and where are auditions?
Vocal auditions are January 8, 2026 from 6:00–9:30pm at the Electric Theater. Dance auditions are January 10, 2026 from 9–11am. Callbacks are by invitation only on January 10, 2026 from 12–2pm.
What should I prepare for the audition?
Prepare 16 bars of a song that best shows off your voice. Bring a physical copy of your headshot and resume.
Can I audition virtually?
Yes. Complete the virtual audition form and upload your video to the submission folder.
What is the participation fee?
The participation fee for Anastasia is $295. Discounts are available — contact us for details.
When are performances?
Performances run March 2–7, 2026 at the Electric Theater in St. George, with several show times across the week. Tickets are here.
