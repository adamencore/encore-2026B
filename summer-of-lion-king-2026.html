About the production

A full production, a real stage, and a whole summer of growth — Summer of The Lion King welcomes first-timers and seasoned young performers alike, building skills through rehearsal and ending with the curtain up.

Registration is split into two age divisions — ages 8–13 and ages 14–18 — so coaching and material fit each group. Choose your division below to register.

FAQ

Questions about Summer of The Lion King

Don't see your question? Call (435) 414-0049 or email hello@encorepa.org.

Who can audition?
Performers ages 8–18 of all experience levels. Registration is split into two divisions — ages 8–13 and ages 14–18 — and auditions are held May 14, 2026 at the Electric Theater.
What is the rehearsal commitment?
Rehearsals run June 1 through July 18, 2026, Monday through Friday, at St. George Academy. Note that rehearsals are at a different location than the performances, which are at the Electric Theater.
What should I prepare for the audition?
Prepare 16 bars (about 45–60 seconds) of a song that showcases your voice, and bring a headshot and resume. No prior experience is required.
How much is the participation fee?
A participation fee applies to take part in the production. Multi-child discounts are available and scholarships can help cover the cost. All fees are non-refundable. See the full breakdown on our participation fee page.
When and where are the performances?
Performances run in late July 2026 at the Electric Theater, 68 E Tabernacle Street in downtown St. George. Exact dates and curtain times are confirmed once casting is set, since times can differ between casts.
Are sensory-friendly or ASL-interpreted performances offered?
Encore regularly offers sensory-friendly performances and select ASL-interpreted shows. Details vary by production — check this page or learn more about our accessibility programs.
