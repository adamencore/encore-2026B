About the Production

Brave, brilliant & a little bit bold

Calling all brave, brilliant, and a little bit rebellious performers. Encore Performing Arts invites young artists ages 11–15 to audition for Roald Dahl's Matilda Jr.

Matilda Jr. is packed with unforgettable songs, big dance numbers, hilarious characters, and a powerful message about courage, kindness, and believing in yourself. Whether you're a seasoned performer or stepping into the spotlight for the first time, we want to see you. Bring your personality, your energy, and your imagination, and come be part of a cast that creates something truly magical onstage. Sometimes you have to be a little bit naughty — and very, very bold.

FAQ

Audition questions

Still have questions? Email hello@encorepa.org or contact us.

Who can audition for Matilda Jr.?
Young performers ages 11–15 are invited to audition. Whether you're a seasoned performer or stepping into the spotlight for the first time, you're welcome.
When and where are auditions?
Vocal auditions are March 18, 2026 from 6:30–9:30pm at the Electric Theater. Dance auditions are March 19, 2026 from 7–8:30pm. Callbacks are by invitation only on March 19 from 8:30–9:30pm.
What should I prepare for the audition?
Prepare 16 bars of a musical song that best shows off your voice. Bring a physical copy of your headshot and resume.
Can I audition virtually?
Yes. Complete the virtual audition form and upload a video of your 16 bars to the vocal folder. A choreography video will be shared so you can submit a dance audition to the choreo folder.
What is the participation fee?
The participation fee for Matilda Jr. is $295. Discounts are available — contact us for details.
When are performances?
Performances run May 6–9, 2026 at the Electric Theater in St. George. Show times will be announced. Tickets are here.
